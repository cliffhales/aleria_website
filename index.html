What Aleria does

Service intake & eligibility

Guide applications, recertifications, and conditional eligibility in one workflow so rider details stay accurate everywhere.

Demand-aware scheduling

Blend standing orders with daily requests, respect vehicles, aides, and time windows, and simulate scenarios before publishing.

Rider & caregiver updates

Offer IVR, SMS, email, and web tracking so people choose how they stay informed. Reduce uncertainty while lowering call volume.

Driver mobile workspace

Give drivers manifests, ADA notes, navigation handoff, and photo/voice notes in an offline-friendly app.

Live ops control center

See trips, vehicles, risks, and incidents in real time. Escalate issues, reassign runs, or trigger support teams in a few clicks.

Program reporting

Track OTP, denials, missed trips, dwell, and slack time. Build shareable dashboards or export raw data to existing BI tools.

Billing & compliance

Capture mileage, fares, funding sources, and incident notes automatically so finance and auditors stay aligned.

Open architecture

APIs, webhooks, and integrations keep Aleria in sync with CAD/AVL, HR/payroll, telephony, and reporting stacks.

Accessibility, privacy, and security

Accessibility

WCAG 2.2 AA patterns, large-type modes, keyboard-and-screen-reader parity, and rider-facing language reviewed by advisory boards.

Privacy

Data minimization, consent and retention controls, and portable exports so agencies stay aligned with policy and grant requirements.

Security

Encryption in transit and at rest, SSO + MFA support, role-based permissions, and full audit logging.

Frequently asked questions

What stage is Aleria currently in?

We're in private pilots with agencies and nonprofits. Feedback from those teams is shaping the first generally-available release.

Can we keep our current phone/SMS provider?

Yes—Aleria plugs into existing telephony and messaging accounts so you can keep familiar numbers, IVR trees, and templates.

Does the driver app work offline?

Yes. Trip events queue locally when coverage drops and sync as soon as the device reconnects, so compliance data stays intact.

What mapping and navigation do you use?

We support commercial and open-source basemaps plus handoff to the navigation apps your fleet already trusts.

How long is implementation?

Most partners move from discovery to pilot service in 6–10 weeks, depending on data migrations, integrations, and training cadence.

Is Aleria cloud-hosted or on-prem?

We offer single-tenant cloud hosting by default and can support dedicated environments that align with your IT policies.

Can we export our data?

Absolutely—bulk exports, APIs, and scheduled drops keep Aleria connected to your reporting or data warehouse stack.
