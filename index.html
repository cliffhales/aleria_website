What Aleria does

Smart Dispatch & Scheduling

Plan efficient routes with rider windows, eligibility, and preferences in mind. Live edits flow to drivers and riders instantly.

Rider Experience

Simple booking and reminders via web/app/SMS/email. Clear pickup windows and updates reduce uncertainty and call volume.

Driver App

Clean manifests, one-tap trip events, optional navigation handoff, and offline resilience for spotty coverage.

Real-time Ops

Live map of vehicles and trips, no-show/late risk flags, and alerts that help you act before small delays become big ones.

Reporting & Insights

See OTP, cancels/no-shows, dwell times, and demand patterns. Export or connect to BI—your data is yours.

Billing & Audit Trail

Accurate trip events and mileage support clean invoicing and transparent audits.

Roles & Permissions

Granular access for dispatchers, drivers, riders, admins, and partners—keep data where it belongs.

APIs & Integrations

Connect messaging, telephony, mapping, and data tools. Bring Aleria into your existing workflows instead of the other way around.

Frequently asked questions

Can we keep our current phone/SMS provider?

Yes—Aleria integrates with common telephony and messaging providers so you can keep existing numbers and templates.

Does the driver app work offline?

Yes—drivers can continue trip events and navigation handoff when coverage is spotty; data syncs when they're back online.

What mapping do you use?

We support multiple map providers and can align to your agency's preference for basemaps and routing.

How long is implementation?

Most teams start pilot operations in weeks, then expand as training and imports complete. We'll shape a plan that matches your readiness.

Is Aleria cloud-hosted or on-prem?

We offer flexible hosting approaches so IT can choose what fits policy and budget.

Can we export our data?

Yes—your data is portable. Use exports or APIs to connect Aleria to your reporting stack.
