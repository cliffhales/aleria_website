Keep every ride clear, from dispatch to drop-off.

Clear Rides is a web-based dispatch and driver platform for demand-response and paratransit systems. Plan trips, assign vehicles, and keep drivers in sync with simple tools your team can actually live in all day.

Clear Rides is designed for 5–50 vehicle demand-response fleets, with a focus on reliability and simple day-to-day operations.

What Clear Rides focuses on first

Start with the daily work that matters most: booking and scheduling trips, dispatching vehicles, and giving drivers a straightforward manifest that works even when connectivity is imperfect.

Dispatch

Plan and adjust your day in one place

Build runs, assign trips, and make same-day changes without fighting the software. Clear Rides Planner is designed for dispatchers who live in the system for an entire shift.

  • Trip entry with clients, addresses, windows, and notes.
  • Assign and rebalance trips across vehicles throughout the day.
  • Track status changes in real time as drivers work their manifests.
Drivers

Manifests that stay clear on the road

Drivers see exactly what they need: who to pick up, where, and when. The mobile app focuses on reliability and low-friction buttons rather than extra clutter.

  • Ordered trip list with names, addresses, and requested times.
  • Simple ARRIVE / NO-SHOW / ONBOARD / DROP-OFF flow.
  • Location updates sent back to dispatch when connectivity allows.
Reporting

Clean data for tomorrow’s reports

Clear Rides starts with practical exports you can use immediately, and leaves room to grow into more advanced reporting over time.

  • Trip- and stop-level CSV exports for analysis.
  • Daily and monthly summaries for internal use.
  • Foundations for regulatory and funding-related reporting.

Two core modules, shared data

Clear Rides starts with two applications that share the same live view of your service: one for the office, one for the road.

Dispatcher

Clear Rides Planner

Web app for schedulers & dispatchers

Plan trips, manage runs, see vehicles on the map, and keep track of what has happened today and what is still at risk.

  • Schedule view by vehicle, by trip, or by time.
  • Live status updates from drivers.
  • Tools for handling no-shows, cancels, and reassignments.
Drivers

Clear Rides Driver

Mobile app for drivers

A driver-focused manifest that keeps working even when coverage is spotty, then syncs back when the connection comes back.

  • See today’s manifest at a glance.
  • Record trip events with a few clear buttons.
  • Launch turn-by-turn navigation to pickup and drop-off locations.

Designed with small and mid-size agencies in mind

Clear Rides focuses first on fleets in roughly the 5–50 vehicle range—large enough to need real tools, small enough that every operator and dispatcher wears multiple hats.

Get off legacy tools

A path forward from aging systems

Move from desktop-only software to a modern web-based platform without trying to replace everything at once.

  • Prioritize the scheduling, dispatch, and driver flows.
  • Plan a staged cutover that matches your risk tolerance.
  • Keep access to historical data while you transition.
Team experience

Built around real dispatcher workflows

The goal is not a flashy interface; it’s a tool dispatchers can live in all day without fighting it.

  • Layout and wording tuned for day-of-service work.
  • Clear, minimal status options instead of long menus.
  • Room to grow into more automation over time.
